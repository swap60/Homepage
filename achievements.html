Awards & Recognitions

🥇

Best Paper Award — International Conference on Computational Mathematics

2024

Awarded for the paper "AI-Driven Optimization of Numerical Viscosity in Shock-Capturing Schemes" at ICCM 2024, Bangalore. Recognized for innovative integration of reinforcement learning with classical numerical methods.

🎖️

Young Scientist Award — Indian Science Congress

2023

Received the Young Scientist Award in the Mathematical Sciences section for contributions to entropy-stable numerical schemes for degenerate parabolic PDEs.

📚

Research Excellence Grant — Amity University

2023

Secured internal research funding of ₹5 Lakhs for the project "Quantum-Inspired Algorithms for Scientific Computing" under the university's Faculty Research Program.

🌐

Visiting Researcher — University of Oxford (Virtual)

2022

Invited as a virtual visiting researcher at the Mathematical Institute, University of Oxford, to collaborate on high-resolution solvers for hyperbolic conservation laws.

⭐

Outstanding Reviewer — Journal of Computational Physics

2022

Recognized as an Outstanding Reviewer for providing thorough, constructive reviews on manuscripts related to numerical methods for PDEs.

🎓

PhD Fellowship — CSIR, Government of India

2018 — 2022

Awarded the prestigious CSIR-JRF (Junior Research Fellowship) and later SRF (Senior Research Fellowship) for doctoral research in computational mathematics.
